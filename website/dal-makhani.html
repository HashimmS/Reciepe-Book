Dal Makhani

A creamy and rich lentil dish cooked with butter and cream, a staple in Indian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Soak the black lentils and kidney beans in water overnight.
  2. Drain and cook them in a pressure cooker with enough water until soft (about 4-5 whistles).
  3. Heat oil and butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  4. Add onions and saut√© until golden brown. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and green chili, cooking for a minute.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook until they break down and become a paste.
  6. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  7. Add the cooked lentils and kidney beans to the tomato mixture. Stir to combine.
  8. Add water if needed to reach desired consistency. Simmer for 20-30 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally.
  9. Stir in the cream and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Adjust salt to taste.
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
