Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or vegetables
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint for garnish
  • Saffron soaked in warm milk

Steps

  1. Cook basmati rice until 70% done and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add sliced onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chicken or vegetables and cook until they turn white.
  5. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, salt, and biryani masala. Mix well.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until the oil separates.
  7. Reduce heat and add yogurt. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  8. Layer the cooked rice over the chicken/vegetable mixture. Sprinkle saffron milk, fresh coriander, and mint leaves on top.
  9. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes until the rice is fully cooked and flavors are absorbed.
  10. Serve hot with raita.
