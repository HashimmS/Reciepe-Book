Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chicken pieces and cook until they turn white.
  4. Add turmeric powder, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  6. Reduce heat and add yogurt. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  7. Add garam masala and simmer for another 10 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
